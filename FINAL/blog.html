Kola in Danger Near You?

FACT: Meaningful legislation is the only way to protect koalas!!

There is currently no legislation, anywhere in the country,... that can protect Koalas and Koala habitat in Australia. The listing of the koala as “vulnerable” under the Environmental Protection and Biodiversity Conservation Act in 2012 changed nothing. This is supposed to be the premier law for protecting Australia’s environment, yet it is powerless.

By the time you read this page the bulldozers may already be working, but it is not too late to take action. Below we have listed some of the things that might make a difference.

Join the Koala Army. The Australian Koala Foundation is committed to the introduction of a Koala Protection Act to provide real legislative protection for koalas, and we need your help.

Media attention can help. Contact your local newspaper. Often they will have an environmental reporter who may be interested in the plight of your Koalas. Write letters to the editor of your local and national newspapers.

Speak to your federal member. The Government needs to hear your story to convince them to protect Koalas and their habitat. More here.
